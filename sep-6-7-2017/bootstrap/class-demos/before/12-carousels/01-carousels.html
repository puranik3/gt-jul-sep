Carousels

Demo A : Usually used with images (though not necessary). Three parts of a carousel are items, indicators and controls.

Classes involved are .carousel, .slide, .carousel-inner, .item, .carousel-caption, ol.carousel-indicators, li.active (indicator and item, .carousel-control, .left, .right. Add .glyphicon-chevron-left, .glyphicon-chevron-right icons for controls - alternatively .icon-prev, .icon-next.

Add data-ride="carousel" to start carousel on load of page, data-interval="time between slides in milliseconds". Indicator li has attributes data-slide-to="slide number", data-target="selector for carousel container". For controls add href="selector for carousel container", data-slide="prev" | "next".
