Forms - Basics

Demo A : Basic set of classes are .form-group, label.control-label and input.form-control. Checkboxes and radio buttons are styled using .checkbox /, .radio, or .checkbox-inline and .radio-inline. Apart from the widths of labels and form controls of form.form-horizontal can be managed using the grid .col-* classes.
Use .disabled for disabling form controls and p.form-control-static for having plain text displayed in place of form control.
